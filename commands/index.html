Bittyconomy Commands

Bittyconomy offers a wide range of commands. It has Six Command Category. Click the category below to view it's command



Economy Commands

B!work

work to earn some money

B!balance

Outputs your balance.

B!shop

Get the items that you can buy.

B!buy

Buy one of the items in the shop

B!crime

commit a crime and earn some money.

B!daily

Claim your daily rewards

B!weekly

Claim your weekly rewards

B!monthly

Claim your monthly rewards

B!pay

Pay the mentioned user the specified amount of money.

B!lock

Put a lock on your wallet, protect yourself from one rob attempted.

B!rob

Rob someone, when the user has a lock equipped it will remove the lock for the next rob attempt.

B!beg

Beg for some money.

B!deposit

Deposit thr specified amount of money in your bank

B!hunt

hunt for some animals.

B!sell

Sell an item for the half the price it's sold in the store for.

B!stats

Get your user stats in the bittyconomy system.

B!lottery

Use a lottery ticket for a small chance to win 40,000 BittyCoins.

B!upgradelist

Get the list of the available bank upgrades.

B!upgradesum

Upgrade your bank with a sum upgrade.

B!upgrademulti

Upgrade your bank with a multiplier. .

B!story

Get the story of bittyconomy, this is needed to understand certain commands.

B!prestige

A fresh start, lose everything for a boost, every prestige increases the boost by one, the price of the prestige also increases.

B!bet

Bet money on a specific spot.

B!slots

A slots machine, you have to specify how much BittyCoins you will use for this, if you win you'll get 5000 BittyCoins, if you lose You'll lose 2000 BittyCoins.

B!quests

Get a list of all quests.

B!claimquest

Claim the rewards of a quest, only works for certain quests.

B!gift

Gift someone an item that you own.

B!moneycrate

Open a money crate


Music Commands

B!play

Play the specified song.

B!stop

Stop playing music and leave the voice channel.

B!skip

Skip the current track and play the next track.

B!pause

Pause the current playing song.

B!resume

Resumed and start playing the paused song.

B!loop

Get a menu where you can choose to loop the queue or loop the song, the bot will await your message so make sure to read the choices it gives.

B!lyrics

Fetch the lyrics of the current playing song.

B!queue

Get the queue, contains thumbnail of the current song, total duration of the song, remaining duration of the song and of course the queue, send the page number after the command to see the page you specified.

B!volume

Change the volume to a max of 150%

B!remove

Remove the specified song from the queue.

Server Settings

B!setprefix

Change the server's prefix for the bot.

B!sjc

Set the mentioned channel to the channel that gets logged with welcome and goodbye messages.

B!chatbot on

Enable chatbot mode. If no channel is mentioned after the command the channel the command is used in will be used as the chatbot channel.

B!chatbot off

Disable chatbot mode.

B!setverifrole

Set the verified role for the server.

B!setcolor

Change the embed color of the embed messages for your server

B!reset

Reset your data, read the policy for info.
